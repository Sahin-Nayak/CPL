Tournament Rules

General Rules

  • Each team must have 3-4 players
  • Matches will be 4 overs per side
  • One bounce one hand catch out is valid
  • No LBW rule applies
  • Maximum 2 overs per bowler

Code of Conduct

  • Respect all players, umpires, and organizers
  • No abusive language or behavior
  • Teams must arrive 10 mins before match time
  • Umpire's decision is final
  • Violations may lead to disqualification
